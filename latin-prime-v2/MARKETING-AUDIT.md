*Expected impact: +10–20% conversion rate on strategy call bookings*

**2. Consolidate to one CTA label sitewide**
Replace all variants ("Book a Demo", "Get Your AI Employee", "Get Started", "Book a Free Demo") with a single consistent label: **"Book Your Free Strategy Call"**. Every button, every section. Decision clarity directly improves click-through.
*Expected impact: +8–12% primary CTA click-through rate*

*Expected impact: Improved entity authority in Google; prerequisite for Knowledge Panel*

*Expected impact: +15–20% continuation rate past mid-funnel*

**7. Add a capacity/scarcity signal to Pricing section**
